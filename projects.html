Along with four fellow classmates I organised and overlooked the first ever interschool computer fest hosted by our school, STSAP Season One - LINK X ( St. Thomas' Symposium And Programming).
LINK X included :

  1. Impeccable Artificers - Logical Reasoning

  2. Decipher X Decode - Programming

  3. Technofrix - Symposium

  4. Infinix - Logo Designing

  5. Extremix - Gaming

  6. The Big Bang Query - Quiz

My part was to design all the rules for different events, gather questions for quiz and logical reasoning, topics for symposium and design the questions for programing.
